Why Minz is Perfect for Indie Authors & Publishers

How reliable QR codes unlock new revenue streams and profitable marketing opportunities for your books

Permanent Links That Never Break

Your printed book is a long-term investment. Unlike free URL shorteners that can disappear overnight, Minz is built for permanence. Your QR codes will work in 20 years, just as reliably as they do today.

Real-World Scenarios:

•Reader picks up your book in a used bookstore in 2045 - QR code still works perfectly
•Library copy gets checked out hundreds of times over decades - links never fail
•Academic citation in research paper links to your supplemental materials forever
•Gift copy passed between family members always provides access to bonus content

The Competition: Free services like bit.ly, tinyurl.com have no guarantee of permanence. When they shut down, your printed QR codes become worthless decoration.
