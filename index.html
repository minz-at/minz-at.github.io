Why Dynamic QR Codes Beat Static QR Codes

Not all QR codes are created equal. Here's why Minz dynamic QR codes are superior to traditional static QR codes for authors and publishers.

Static QR Codes

Traditional QR code generators create static codes that can never be changed once printed.

  • Can't update destination after printing
  • Broken links mean reprinting costs
  • No analytics or reader insights
  • Lost revenue from outdated promotions
  • No content protection or expiration control

Minz Dynamic QR Codes

Smart QR codes that redirect through Minz, allowing you to update destinations anytime.

  • Update destinations anytime, anywhere
  • Never reprint books or marketing materials
  • Detailed reader analytics and insights
  • Maximize revenue with fresh promotions
  • Password protection and campaign controls

Real-World Publishing Example

❌ With Static QR Codes:

You print 1,000 copies of your book with a QR code linking to your website. Six months later, you:

  • • Want to promote your new book instead

  • • Have a limited-time book sale

Result: You can't change anything. The QR codes are useless.

✅ With Minz Dynamic QR Codes:

You print 1,000 copies with a Minz QR code. Six months later, you simply:

  • • Track exactly how many readers engage

Result: Your printed books keep generating revenue forever.
