About Minz

Minz is a dynamic QR code platform designed specifically for authors and publishers. We solve the fundamental problem of static QR codes in printed books: once printed, they can never be changed.

With Minz, creators can update their QR code destinations anytime—transforming printed books from frozen snapshots into living gateways that connect readers to evolving content, communities, and experiences.

Never spend $3,000-$10,000 on reprinting books because of broken QR codes

20+ year longevity that matches your book's shelf life—set it and forget it

No technical skills required—if you can copy a link, you can use Minz
