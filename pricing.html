Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and features

What happens after my free trial?

After 30 days, you'll be charged for your selected plan. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charges.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do my QR codes stop working if I cancel?

No! Your QR codes will continue redirecting to their last destination. You just can't update them until you reactivate your account.

Is there a setup fee or contract?

No setup fees, no contracts. Our pricing is simple and transparent. Pay month-to-month and cancel anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. All transactions are secured with enterprise-grade encryption.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?

Yes! We offer special pricing for nonprofits, educational institutions, and students. Contact us for details.
